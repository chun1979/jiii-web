Review & Editorial Policy

Our commitment to quality and the peer review process.

Editorial Policy

The Editors on the Editorial Board for Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure are committed to maintaining the academic quality of the proceedings and to fostering the development of interdisciplinary and cutting-edge research areas. To this end, all submitted manuscripts are subject to a rigorous peer-review process. Based on the evaluation results, the Editorial Board determines the acceptability of each manuscript.

Peer Review Procedure

Reviewers are requested to conduct the review in accordance with the provided review system (ScholarOne). However, in recognition of the emerging nature of the field, excessive emphasis on the completeness or polish of the submission should be avoided to prioritize innovation.

  • Constructive Feedback: Reviewers are asked to ensure that comments are constructive and clearly stated.
  • Confidentiality: The names of individual reviewers will not be disclosed to the author or to any external parties under any circumstances.

Final Decision on Acceptance

The final decision on acceptance or rejection, including any revision requests, will be made by the Editorial Board based on the outcome of the review and communicated to the author under the Editorsâ€™ name.

Handling of Previously Published Work

Manuscripts containing previously published content may be accepted if they have been substantially restructured to incorporate new findings or perspectives. The editorial board will assess whether the submission presents new knowledge, and authors are required to cite any previously published material accurately.
