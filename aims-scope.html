Aims & Scope

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure is an international Open Access journal published by the Japan Society of Civil Engineers (JSCE).

Scope

The journal serves as a core academic platform for research dissemination in AI and data science, with particular emphasis on their practical applications in civil engineering.

  • AI & Machine Learning in Civil Engineering
  • Digital Twins & Infrastructure Management
  • Smart Cities & Urban Data Science
  • Robotics & Construction Automation
  • Disaster Prevention Informatics

Article Types

Original Research Papers, Review Articles, and Technical Reports.
