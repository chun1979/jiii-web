Aims & Scope

Defining the intersection of AI, Data Science, and Civil Engineering.

Innovation and social implementation of AI, data science, and digital twins are advancing rapidly in a wide range of fields including infrastructure, disaster prevention, cities, environment, transportation, and energy.

Scope of the Journal

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure solicits papers from research and development, education, survey and testing, to practices in the related areas. We aim to serve as a core academic platform for research dissemination in AI and data science.

Article Types
