Aims & Scope

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure is an international, open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science within the Civil Engineering and Infrastructure sectors.

Scope of the Journal

The journal serves as a core academic platform for research dissemination in AI and data science, with particular emphasis on their practical applications in civil engineering. We welcome contributions that bridge the gap between advanced informatics technologies and physical infrastructure systems.

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • AI and Machine Learning applications in structural health monitoring
  • Digital Twins for infrastructure management and maintenance
  • Data-driven disaster prevention and mitigation
  • Smart city technologies and urban planning
  • Robotics and automation in construction
  • Big data analysis in transportation and logistics

Audience

Our audience includes researchers, engineers, and practitioners involved in civil engineering, computer science, and data analysis who are interested in the interdisciplinary fusion of these fields.

Article Types

The journal accepts Original Research Papers, Review Articles, and Technical Reports. All submissions must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal.
