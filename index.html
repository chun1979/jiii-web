Bridging AI, Data Science and Infrastructure

The official journal of the Japan Society of Civil Engineers (JSCE). We publish cutting-edge research regarding the application of AI, IoT, and Data Science in civil engineering, prioritizing innovation and social relevance.

Aims & Scope

Technology of AI and data science is rapidly developing and expanding to various fields of application. Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure serves as a core academic platform for research dissemination, from fundamental development to practical implementation in infrastructure maintenance, disaster prevention, and smart cities.
