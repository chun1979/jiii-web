About the Journal

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure (JIII) is a peer-reviewed open-access journal that publishes original research papers, reviews, and technical reports. We aim to provide a platform for researchers and practitioners to discuss the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI), IoT, and Data Science in the maintenance, management, and innovation of civil infrastructure.

Scopus & WoS Target

We adhere to high ethical standards (COPE) and rigorous peer review to meet the criteria for future inclusion in Scopus and Web of Science.
