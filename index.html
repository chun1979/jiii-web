About the Journal

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure (JIII) solicits papers in the related area of artificial intelligence and data science including research and development, education, survey and testing, and practices.

Technology of AI and data science is rapidly developing and expanding to various fields of application. We serve as a core academic platform for research dissemination, prioritizing innovation, future potential, and social relevance.
