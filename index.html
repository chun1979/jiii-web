About the Journal

Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure (JIII) is a peer-reviewed open-access journal published by the Japan Society of Civil Engineers (JSCE). It is dedicated to the intersection of artificial intelligence, informatics, and infrastructure engineering.

Open Access & Archiving

This journal is fully Open Access. All articles are published under the CC BY 4.0 license, allowing free access and reuse. Archives are maintained in J-STAGE and the National Diet Library.

Revenue Sources

JIII is supported by Article Processing Charges (APCs) and the budget of the Committee on Structural Engineering, JSCE. We do not accept commercial advertising.
