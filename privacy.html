Privacy Policy

The Intelligence, Informatics and Infrastructure (JIII) and the Japan Society of Civil Engineers (JSCE) are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Collection of Information

We collect personal information (names, affiliations, email addresses) provided by authors, reviewers, and editors solely for the purpose of processing manuscripts and publishing the journal.

2. Use of Information

The collected information will be used strictly for:

  • Managing the peer-review process.
  • Communicating editorial decisions.
  • Publishing accepted articles (author names and affiliations will be made public).
  • Sending necessary notifications regarding the journal.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except for trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website (e.g., J-STAGE, ScholarOne), conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at:
