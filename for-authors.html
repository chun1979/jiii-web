Information for Authors

Submission Overview

Manuscripts should be written in English and submitted via the ScholarOne Manuscripts system. All submissions are subject to a rigorous peer-review process.

Article Processing Charges (APC)

Publication Fee: 27,500 JPY (tax included).
The fee covers the costs of peer review, editing, and open-access publication. Waivers may be considered under special circumstances.

Peer Review Process

We employ a rigorous peer-review process to ensure the high quality and validity of published research.

  1. Initial Screening: The Editor-in-Chief performs an initial check for scope and format compliance.
  2. Reviewer Assignment: Manuscripts are assigned to at least two independent experts in the field.
  3. Evaluation: Reviewers evaluate the paper based on originality, technical accuracy, clarity, and contribution to the field.
  4. Decision: Based on the reviewers' reports, the Editorial Board makes a final decision: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject.

Manuscript Formatting

Please use the official template below for preparing your manuscript.

  • Font: Times New Roman (Title: 18pt, Authors: 12pt, Main text: 11pt)
  • Layout: Double column for main text, Single column for title/abstract.
  • Abstract: Max 300 words.
  • Key Words: About 5 words.
