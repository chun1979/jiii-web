Article Processing Charges (APC)

Publication Fee: 27,500 JPY (tax included).
This fee supports peer review, system maintenance, and open-access publication costs.

Peer Review Process

  1. Screening: Initial check by Editor-in-Chief.
  2. Review: Blind review by at least two independent experts.
  3. Decision: Final decision by the Editorial Board based on reviewer reports.
